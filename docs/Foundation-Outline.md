## Purpose

The **Foundation Statement** is the canonical document of the **Temporal Liquidity Market (TLM)** project.

Its purpose is to establish the conceptual foundations, terminology, design principles, and long-term research agenda for temporal market design in blockchain protocols.

Rather than proposing a single protocol mechanism, the Foundation Statement defines the common language and research framework upon which future mechanism proposals, simulations, protocol specifications, and implementations can be developed.

The Foundation Statement should therefore be regarded as the constitutional document of the TLM project.

# Writing Principles

The Foundation Statement follows several principles.

* Introduce concepts before proposing mechanisms.
* Build upon existing Ethereum protocol research.
* Preserve participant neutrality and decentralized incentives.
* Distinguish conceptual foundations from implementation details.
* Encourage open discussion through clearly stated assumptions, scope, and open questions.
